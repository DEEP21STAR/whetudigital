💡 Smart Tips
💜
Budget Wizard makes it simple to track spending, hit savings goals, and stay on top of subscriptions — all in one place.
🚀 Getting started
1
Add your expenses as they happen — even quick ones. Small amounts add up!
2
Check your Spending Rings daily. A ring near full means a category is running low.
3
Set up Savings Goals for things you're working towards — holiday, gadget, emergency fund.
4
Log all subscriptions so nothing surprises you at month end.
5
Check Insights weekly — the bar chart and tips help you spot patterns.
📊 Features
💍
Spending Rings
Apple Watch-style rings show how much of each category budget you've used. Green = good, red = over.
🎯
Savings Goals
Track progress toward each goal with animated progress bars. Add contributions anytime.
🔄
Subscriptions
See all recurring costs, total monthly/annual spend, and upcoming billing dates.
📈
Insights
Category breakdown chart + month-by-month spending history. Smart tips based on your data.
🏆
Streaks & Badges
Stay under budget 7 days in a row to build a streak. Earn badges for milestones.
⬇
CSV Export
Download all expenses as a spreadsheet for your records or accountant.
