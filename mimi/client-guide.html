Reading your weekly plan

Each week starts on Monday. The plan shows your training days, your rest days, and a short note from Mimi about the focus for that week.

Each training day has 3-6 exercises. Tap any exercise to see how to do it - there is a short demo video, three form cues, and one common mistake to avoid. The video shows you exactly what good form looks like.

Reps and weight are starting points. If something feels too easy or too hard, log what you actually did. Mimi adjusts your plan based on what you log, not what was on paper.

You decide the orderIf a day says "squat, then bench, then row" and you want to bench first because the squat rack is busy - do that. The order matters less than getting it done.

What the streak means

Your streak counts how many weeks in a row you have done at least 2 sessions. Miss a week, streak resets to zero. Hit 4 weeks, you unlock a small celebration screen. Hit 12, you get a free month review with Mimi.

The streak is there because consistency beats intensity. The strongest woman you know is not the one who killed two sessions and quit - she is the one who kept showing up.

Life happensYou can pause your streak for one week per quarter for things like work travel, illness, or just needing a break. Tap "Pause week" - your streak holds.

How form-check works

Open any session. Tap the camera icon next to a lift. Record yourself doing the exercise (10-15 seconds is enough). Upload.

The system reads your movement using your phone's camera, compares it to ideal form for that lift, and gives you 2-3 specific cues. Examples:

  • "Your knees are caving in at the bottom of the squat - drive them out as you stand"
  • "Hips are rising before shoulders on the deadlift - keep your back flat and push the floor away"
  • "Bar path is drifting forward on the press - bring your head back through at lockout"

It's not magic and it's not perfect - it catches the big stuff. For anything subtle, send Mimi the video and she will look at it personally.

Messages with Mimi

The message button is in the bottom right of every screen. Tap it to write to Mimi directly. She gets the message instantly. You will hear back the same day - usually within 2-3 hours during her working hours.

Use messages for: questions about a lift, feeling stuck, life events that affect training (new job, holiday, period symptoms), asking for adjustments.

For emergencies (injury, severe pain, can't make a session), call Mimi - the phone icon next to the message button.

Your progress page

Every month, you get a personal progress summary. It shows:

  • Your strongest lifts vs 90 days ago
  • Body composition trends (if you logged them)
  • How many sessions you did vs your plan
  • Mimi's note on what you crushed and what to focus on next month

The progress page is private to you. Mimi sees a different version for coaching decisions, but your personal page is yours alone.
