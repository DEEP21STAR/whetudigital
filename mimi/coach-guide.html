DAY 01

Set up your studio profile and PIN

Open the dashboard. First time only, you'll be asked to set a 4-digit PIN. Make it something only you and your phone know. The PIN never leaves your device - it's stored locally, no server, no leak risk.

Fill in: studio name (default: "Train with Mimi"), location (Aotearoa NZ), and your booking email. The booking email is what shows on the public services page when someone clicks "Book a free consult".

Why this mattersOnce your PIN is set, every refresh skips the login screen until you sign out. Saves you 8 seconds every morning - that's an hour a year.
DAY 02

Add your first client and program

Click Clients in the side nav. Click + Add client top-right. Required fields: name, email, phone, primary goal. Optional but recommended: starting weight, training experience, any injuries.

Once the client is saved, click their card and hit Program builder. Drag exercises from the library onto the week-view. Each exercise has form cues, a common-mistake note, and a women-specific cue baked in - tap any exercise to expand.

TipStart simpler than you think. A 4-exercise StrongHer template beats an 8-exercise plan they'll never finish. The library is sorted by difficulty (1-5 dots) - keep newbies on 1-2.
DAY 03

Read your morning AI brief

Every morning at 6:00 NZ time, the dashboard generates a "What should I do today" brief. It runs as a quiet background job - no email spam.

The brief gives you 3-5 prioritised actions for the day. Examples it surfaces:

  • "Hana hasn't booked next month - send rebook prompt"
  • "Sophie hit her 100kg deadlift milestone yesterday - one-click celebrate"
  • "Revenue dropped 18% this week vs last - 2 candidate reasons"

You can ignore, snooze, or one-click execute each action. The brief learns from what you accept vs ignore - within 30 days it gets specific to how you actually coach.

Don't trust blindlyThe AI is suggesting, not deciding. If a "send rebook prompt" doesn't feel right because Hana told you in person she's pausing, just dismiss it. The AI won't text anyone without your tap.
DAY 04

Send a branded email or SMS

Two channels: email (free, longer) and SMS (paid ~5c NZ each, instant). Both share the same template library and personalisation tokens.

To send: Comms tab > pick template (Welcome / Week-1 check-in / Plateau pep-talk / Win-back / etc.) > pick the client > preview > send.

Personalisation tokens auto-fill from client data: {first_name}, {last_session}, {streak_weeks}, {next_session_day}. Drop them anywhere in the body.

Inbound replies land in Comms > Inbox. Threaded by client. Email inbound is free (Gmail relay). SMS inbound is the same cost as outbound (paid).

Cost disciplineUse email for nurture, SMS only for time-sensitive: booking reminders, missed-session prompts, and promo broadcasts. SMS broadcast to 50 clients = $2.50 NZ - worth it for a fill-the-gap promo.
DAY 05

Launch a promo broadcast

Promos live in Comms > Broadcast. The flow:

  1. Segment - tag-based filter (new lead / active / paused / inactive). Or hand-pick.
  2. Template - pick from the promo library. Tokens auto-fill per recipient.
  3. Channel - email, SMS, or both. SMS shows cost preview before send.
  4. Timing - "send now" or "AI-suggest best time". The AI looks at when each client typically replies (mornings vs evenings) and schedules per-person.
  5. Preview - shows the message as the first 3 recipients will see it, with their personal tokens filled in.
  6. Send - one tap. Real-time delivery + reply tracking starts immediately.

Promo templates that work seasonally: school holidays = "fit-mum reset", post-NYE = "January transform", mid-winter = "indoor strength", spring = "wedding-prep 12-week".

REFERENCE

Every dashboard panel, explained.

Active Clients - count of clients with status = active. Excludes paused, archived, churned. The trend below shows the count change vs 30 days ago.

Sessions This Week - count of completed + booked sessions Mon-Sun. The dotted line is the current day - filled bars are done, dashed bars are upcoming.

Monthly Revenue - sum of paid invoices in the current calendar month. Pulled from Stripe Connect once wired. Until then: marked SAMPLE.

Avg Progress - composite score: streak, strength gain, body comp shift, retention. Capped at 100. The trend uses your last 90 days.

Weekly Sessions chart - same data as the KPI, but per-day bars. Hover to see client names.

Revenue Trend chart - rolling 6-month revenue. Inflection points (sharp dips/peaks) get auto-flagged with a small dot for "what changed".

Today's Sessions - real-time. Click any session row to open the client's program, then mark sets done on the fly.

Streak - days in a row you logged at least one session. Resets at midnight if zero sessions. Decorative + motivational - doesn't affect any data.

HELP

When something feels wrong

If a number looks off, the dashboard has a verification path. Click any KPI > "Show source data". You'll see the raw rows that built the number. If those look wrong, the integration is the issue, not the dashboard.

If a client says they didn't get an email/SMS you sent: Comms > History > click the send event > see delivery status (sent / opened / clicked / bounced).

For anything stuck, open Telegram and message Deep - the system pushes alerts to deepstar-phoenix automatically when missions fail.

You are not on your ownEvery Saturday morning, the system runs a self-audit and emails you a "what changed this week" summary. If you ever wonder "did anything break?" - check Sunday inbox first.
