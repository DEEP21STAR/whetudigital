5 Common Questions

❓ The screen has flowers and music — is that normal?

Yes, completely normal. When a guest opens the invitation link, a 6-second blessing animation plays with golden petals. It will continue on its own, or they can tap Skip.

❓ What if a guest changes their mind about coming?

They can open the same invitation link again and choose a different answer. There is a "Go back" button on the confirmation screen.

❓ I cannot see the website — what do I do?

The server needs to be running on the computer. Open PowerShell and type:

node C:\SALINA\_server.js

Or simply call Sachind on 021 293 5401.

❓ What if a guest does not have WhatsApp?

They can use the SMS button to send a text message instead, or use the Email button, or just call Sachind directly.

❓ What if the text is too small?

Tap the "Bigger Text" button at the top of the invitation page. It makes all text 1.5× larger.
