📱 Send SMS from Desktop (no WhatsApp needed)

Windows Phone Link — free, built into Windows 11

Works with any Android phone. Once set up, you can send & receive SMS from your desktop.

Step 1: Press Win key → search "Phone Link" → open it
Step 2: On your Android phone → install "Link to Windows" app (Google Play)
Step 3: Scan the QR code shown in Phone Link on your PC with the app
Step 4: Accept the Bluetooth pairing → done. Messages tab appears in Phone Link
Step 5: Click any SMS button below to pre-fill the invitation → it opens in Phone Link or your default SMS app

Alternative: If you use Google Messages on Android → open messages.google.com/web in Brave → scan QR → send SMS from browser.
